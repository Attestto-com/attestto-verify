Sign a Document

Sign with your DID wallet or browser key — your file never leaves your device.

How it works

Step 1

Connect

Your DID wallet is auto-detected, or generate a browser key instantly.

Step 2

Drop a PDF

The file stays on your device. We compute a SHA-256 hash locally.

Step 3

Sign

A W3C Verifiable Credential is created and stored in your wallet.

DID Wallet Recommended

Connect Attestto ID or any CHAPI-compatible wallet. Your signature is tied to your decentralized identity.

Browser Key Instant

No wallet? Generate a self-issued did:key in your browser. Works immediately, portable later.

Firma Digital Coming soon

Sign with your Costa Rica Firma Digital smart card. PKI-backed, legally binding.

Verify after signing

Every signed document can be verified at verify.attestto.com — no install, no account.
